About Me

I'm an engineer with a keen interest in AI - its power to reshape industries, the ethical questions it raises, and the opportunities it holds for the future.

At present, I work as a Software Engineer with Channelscaler, a leading AI-powered PRM platform. Previous experience includes freelance work with Data Annotation Tech, performing prompt engineering and data annotation to train programming chatbots and AI agents through RLHF.
